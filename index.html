About

I am a PhD Candidate at FLAME Lab and MATRIX Lab in Department of Computational and Data Sciences , IISc . I pursued my masters in Aerospace engineering from IIT Kanpur . My resaerch work is multiscale modelling of reactive molecular dynamics simulation based on quantum mechanical calculation of Hydrogen combustion in equilibrium and nonequilibrium conditions. I have succesfully completed Pin Bhabha pass trek, Himachal Pradesh, which is 4896 meters altitude. I find my peace and motivation in running. As a part of it, I have recently completed half marathon conducted by SkyUltra. I love watching Anime.
